Q. Do children get any discount on IRCTC Train Tickets?
A. IRCTC doesn't charge below the age of 5. for children who are above 5 years, you'd have to pay the full ticket fare, and they will be provided with a seat.




Q. can i book a tatkal ticket?
A. yes, you can book your tatkal ticket on MakeMyTicket in case of emergencies or last-minute travel planes. the tatkal window opens for an hour.
